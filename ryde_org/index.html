Features of Our Training Program

The target Segment of our training programs are Rural Youth. Our programs ensure professional and complete development of our students to get Jobs. We teach aptitude, Presentation and Technical skills. We track and monitor growth of every student until they get Jobs.

learn more
Cost of Training

For the College students we conduct training free of Cost. For students who are working and in a job the fee is Rs 500/- Per Month.

Flexi Timing

We offer flexible time schedule based on the need of the students. The class timings are schedule between 2.00 PM to 9.00 Pm on the weekdays

Expert Trainers

Our talented and resolute team of trainers believe in imparting knowledge to students rather than just teaching.

Curriculum

Our curriculum is packaged with product features, Business Scenarios, case study and handson Activities.

Mode of Delivery

The teaching sessions are Organized in our Rural training centre and online.

certificate

We issue participation Certificate to all Our students for the Subjects they complete.
